Support

We're a small team of musicians — we read everything.

Contact us

For questions, bug reports, feature requests, account help, or anything else, email us at bandframeapp@gmail.com. We aim to reply within a couple of days.

Subscription help

Subscriptions are billed and managed through your app store account. To cancel or change your plan, open your device's subscription settings (Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on iOS). Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple through reportaproblem.apple.com.

Delete your account

You can permanently delete your account and all your data any time from inside the app: Profile → Delete Account. If you can't access the app, email us and we'll do it for you.

Privacy questions

See our Privacy Policy for what we collect and your rights, or email us to exercise a data request.
