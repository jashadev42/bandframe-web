# Privacy Policy

**BandFrame**
Last updated: July 3, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

BandFrame ("we", "our", or "us") is a music creation and collaboration app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using BandFrame, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address** — used to create and authenticate your account
- **Display name** — your name shown to other users in the app
- **Username** — your unique @handle used for search and mentions
- **Profile photo** — an optional photo you choose to upload. If you set one, we access only the photo you select from your photo library — never your other photos.
- **Bio and instruments** — optional details you add to your public profile

### Content You Create
- **Guitar, bass, ukulele, and piano tabs** — the musical notation you write in the app
- **Song sections, lyrics, and project settings** — all project data you create
- **Audio recordings** — vocal takes, instrument recordings, and beatbox recordings you record through the app
- **Imported audio files** — files you import from your device into the Audio Library

### Usage Data
- **Crash reports** — if the app crashes or encounters an error, we receive a technical report so we can fix the problem. Crash reports may include what screen you were on and what action triggered the error. They do not include your recordings, tabs, or messages.

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

### Device Information
- **Device type and OS version** — included in crash reports to help us reproduce and fix bugs (e.g. "iPhone 14, iOS 18").

We do not collect your location, contacts, or any data from other apps.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and maintain your account
- Provide the core features of the app (tab writing, recording, collaboration)
- Enable band collaboration features (sharing projects with other users)
- Send in-app notifications (band invites, activity updates)
- Allow other users to find you by username or display name
- Respond to support requests

We do **not** use your data for advertising. We do **not** sell your data to third parties.

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### With Other Users
When you participate in band collaboration features, other members of your band project can see:
- Your display name and username
- Your profile photo
- Content you contribute to shared projects (tabs, recordings)

Your email address is never visible to other users.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. Your tabs, projects, and profile data are protected by per-user access rules enforced at the database level. Audio files are stored at long, unguessable links — only people you share a project with are given those links through the app. We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.

Passwords are never stored by us — authentication is handled by Supabase Auth using secure hashing.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all of your data is permanently deleted including:

- Your profile and account information
- All projects, sections, and tabs you created
- All audio recordings and library files
- Your follows, notifications, and activity history

This deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you
- **Correction** — update your display name, username, and profile photo at any time in the app
- **Deletion** — delete your account and all associated data from within the app (Settings → Delete Account)
- **Portability** — request an export of your data by contacting us

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you additionally have the rights provided by the GDPR, including the right to object to processing and to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. If you are a California resident, you have the rights provided by the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to deletion. We do not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

## 8. Children's Privacy

BandFrame is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
