Features

A full songwriting studio in your pocket

BandFrame replaces the pile of voice memos, notes-app lyrics, and half-remembered riffs with one place your whole band can work from.

Write real tabs

A fast, touch-first TAB editor for guitar, bass, and ukulele — with per-bar subdivisions, repeats, alternate tunings, and playback that plays your tab back at tempo.

Record every idea

Capture vocal and instrumental takes right in the app with backing tracks and metronome. Heart your favorites and add notes so the good take never gets lost.

Arrange in the Studio

Drag your takes onto a multi-track timeline. Per-track volume, mute, and solo. Record new layers while the timeline plays — build a demo without leaving your phone.

Built for bands

Invite your bandmates to a project with a link. Everyone can add parts, hear the latest takes, and stay in sync — with per-member edit or view-only access.

Fretboard & chord tools

Plot shapes on an interactive fretboard with automatic chord detection, strum and arpeggio preview, plus a built-in metronome.

Everywhere you are

Your projects live in the cloud. Pro backs up every recording automatically, exports full-score PDFs of your songs, and keeps playing when your phone is locked.
